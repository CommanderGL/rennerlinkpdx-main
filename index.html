About Me

I am currently a senior in high school near Portland, Oregon. I have always been a builder, visualizing projects in 3D and then making them come to life with blocks or LEGO® products. Along with my own creations, I've always had a fascination with building design and enjoy exploring and photographing the urban Portland landscape. In 2023, I was able to participate in the ACE Mentorship Program and take a course in engineering and design. This opened my eyes to a career in architecture. When I am not building new creations or documenting my builds for my Youtube channel, I am exploring Portland on my bike or playing soccer.
